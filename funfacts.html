  • Dolphins are highly intelligent and social animals.
  • They communicate using a variety of sounds, including clicks and whistles.
  • Dolphins can swim up to 60 km/h (37 mph) in short bursts.
  • They have a unique way of hunting called fish herding.
  • Dolphins can recognize themselves in mirrors, indicating self-awareness.
